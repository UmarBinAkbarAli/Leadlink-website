№ 04 · The Record

Questions editors of this page get asked

No honest PR firm can — editorial decisions belong to editors, and anyone promising a guaranteed Forbes feature is selling you a paid advertorial dressed up as earned media. What we do control is the quality of the story, the strength of the angle, and the relationships the pitch travels through. That's why our placement rate is what it is.

Typically within the first 30–60 days for press releases and podcast bookings, and 30–90 days for major publication features, depending on editorial calendars and the strength of your story. The first two weeks are spent on the story audit — skipping that step is why most PR fails.

Every business that has survived has a story — most founders are just too close to see it. Contrarian market takes, unusual data you're sitting on, the origin nobody asked about, a customer transformation worth documenting. Finding that angle is precisely the job. If after the audit we genuinely don't see one, we'll tell you that too.

Earned. We pitch journalists and editors on stories worth covering — we don't buy advertorials and pass them off as press. Where a sponsored opportunity genuinely makes sense for your goals, we'll present it transparently labeled as exactly what it is, and you decide.
